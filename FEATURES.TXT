Part 1 
A level features'
Commands:
GO  – The player can go in any direction
HELP – Gives the player their command options
QUIT – Stops the game.
PICK [item] – Allows the player to pick up an item
DROP [item] – Allows the player to drop an item
INVENTORY – Allows the player to store multiple items

Rooms:

Multiple rooms with various items for the player to explore

Inventory Management:

Allows the player to hold their items

Room History:

Player can retrace their steps and visit a room they just left

Extra Credit Features:

Player Class for Inventory Management:

Player class to hold the inventory information for the user, thus making the inventory code easily accessible and usable in other classes.
